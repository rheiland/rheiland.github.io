Building VTK

export BOOST_ROOT=/full-path-to/boost_1_40_0_build

cd /path-to-vtk-build/
cmake /Users/heiland/Documents/workspace/VTK/
or, if you prefer ccmake:
ccmake /Users/heiland/Documents/workspace/VTK/

turn on shared libs, examples, BOOST, PARALLEL, VTK_USE_64BIT_IDS
CARBON ON
COCOA OFF 
configure... (e.g. 'cmake .')
turn on  USE_MPI
configure... (e.g. 'cmake .')
turn on VTK_USE_PARALLEL_BGL
configure... (e.g. 'cmake .')

optionally, build with:
VTK_USE_MYSQL:BOOL=ON

I also do:
CMAKE_BUILD_TYPE:STRING=Debug

DYLD_LIBRARY_PATH=.:/Users/heiland/dev/boost_1_40_0_build/lib:/Users/heiland/dev/Lumsdaine/Sandia/build-8-27-09/bin:(other stuff)
