Tiled Walls

Tiled walls are just that - a large "wall" of computer monitors (typically driven by a computer cluster). I was part of the Vis & Virtual Environments team that assembled NCSA's first (8x5) tiled wall. The software used to drive the wall was Chromium.


Above: Projectors for NCSA's tiled wall (photos courtesy Paul Rajlich). Also see demo pages for SC01 and SC02.
